**Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**


* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
* Do not share personal information on unsecured websites
* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone
* Always verify payment requests before approving them
* Do not scan unknown QR codes for receiving money
* Use only trusted apps for online transactions
* Keep your social media accounts private and secure
* Be cautious while accepting friend requests from strangers
* Do not share sensitive photos, documents, or location publicly
* Report cyber fraud on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
* Call Helpline Number: 1930 in case of online financial fraud

Social Media Do's and Don'ts


DO's:
1. Maintain a professional and clean profile
2. Use respectful and appropriate language
3. Protect your personal and private information
4. Verify information before sharing it
5. Be mindful of your digital footprint
6. Use strong passwords and privacy settings

DON'Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
2. Do not post inappropriate or offensive content
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or harmful behavior
4. Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
5. Do not spread fake news or misinformation
6. Do not overshare personal details online
